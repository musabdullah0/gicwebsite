Welcome to Georgetown Islamic Center

The Georgetown Islamic Center serves the Muslim community in Round Rock and surrounding areas, providing a place for worship, education, and community gathering.

Our mission is to foster a strong Islamic identity while promoting understanding, peace, and service to all members of our community.

  • Daily five-time prayers
  • Friday Jummah prayers
  • Islamic education programs
  • Community events and activities
  • Youth programs
